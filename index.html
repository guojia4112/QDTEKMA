Method & Data

Trans-EKMA Framework

Trans-EKMA uses a photochemical box model to simulate ozone formation under combinations of VOC and NOx emissions. These simulations generate background VOC and NOx isopleths, forming a modeled NOx–O₃ diagnostic space. Routine observations of NOx and the daily ozone amplitude are then placed onto this diagram to determine whether ozone formation is NOx-limited or VOC-limited, and to visualize how precursor conditions may have shifted over time.

Qingdao Implementation

This Qingdao version applies the Trans-EKMA framework using local meteorology (pressure, temperature, humidity, boundary-layer height) and regional VOC reactivity profiles. City-average observations (2021–2025) are overlaid to show regimes and trends.
